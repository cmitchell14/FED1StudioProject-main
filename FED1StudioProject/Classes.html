Class Subjects

Fishing

We offer fishing classes of all kinds. We can provide you with the equipment, or show you how to devise your own in the wild. Either way, we will ensure that your hook will not go unused.

Fire Building

The most primal of experiences, and a key to surviving in the wild. Learn to build a fire using primitive tools. Our classes cover a wide range of techniques and in what regions to use them.

Backpacking

One of our favorite pastimes, and a great start for any beginner striving to be a survival expert. Learn the ins and outs of navigating rough terrain, and trekking using only a map and compass for guidance.

Hunting

This isn't your normal hunting class. Learn to camoulflage like a pro, and build, and utilize, your own primitive weapons. Learn the basics of tracking prey and prepping your kill.

Shelter Building

One of the most imporant things you need to know to survive, is how to shelter yourself properly. We'll teach you to build primitive shelters in different regions.

Rock Climbing

Everyone's favorite class: rock climbing. Start with our ropes courses, and work your way up to freestyle rockclimbing. Learn the techniques to scale any peak.
